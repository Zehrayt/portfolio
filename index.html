About Me

I am a passionate and curious individual committed to growing in the fields of technology and engineering. From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by how things work — from circuit boards to code. Over time, this curiosity turned into hands-on experience as I began developing projects in cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and game development. I thrive in collaborative environments where innovative ideas are welcomed, and I enjoy solving complex problems with practical and creative solutions. My participation in various national competitions has not only strengthened my technical skills but also helped me build resilience, communication, and leadership. My goal is to become a well-rounded engineer who contributes to building a smarter, safer, and more connected world.
